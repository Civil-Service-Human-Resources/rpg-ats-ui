You are eligible to apply via the Guaranteed Interview Scheme if you have a disability as defined by the Equality Act 2010. This means you must have:

  • A physical or mental impairment which has a substantial and long term (over 12 months) adverse effect on your ability to carry out normal day to day activities; or
  • A long term health condition; and
  • Demonstrated that you meet the minimum qualifying criteria set out in the advertisement for the post at the application and testing stages.
